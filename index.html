Campus Life

School Gymnasium

Was Established In 1967

The Gymnasiam, also called a Gym is just a regular gym but at the end of every month they put hundreds of chairs on the floor and make a mini-auditorium for "super-student" awards and school-plays. after school they always have desks the custodians set up after school for the after school kids to continue learning.

School Library

Was Established In 1967

The library is just a regular library with tons of seats and even more books. There Is Also a Kind librarian, Ms. Ross. It is also a place where student sometimes may go to learn and do work as well, teachers also do read alouds to everyone at the library, whoever wants to go can go during lunchtime with there lunches, it is very fun!

School Park

Was Established In 1967

The School Park Is A Huge Park With Multiple Enormous Segments/Peices of equipments that consists of different types of swings and mutliple long, steep slides. it also had many more things that regular and not-regular parks have, the park is AWSOME!!!
